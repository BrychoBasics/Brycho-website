Survival Guide

The tenth world is unforgiving. As you venture out of the Meadows and into the Black Forest, you'll need to prepare better food and craft stronger armor to withstand the harsh environments.

Creature Combat Tactics

It is crucial to learn the vulnerabilities of the beasts you hunt. For instance, bears possess thick hides that resist blunt force, making clubs entirely ineffective. Their primary weakness is pierce damage, so relying on bows with flint or better arrows, or a well-timed spear thrust, will give you the upper hand in a forest encounter.

The Swamps

A foul stench permeates the air. Draugr patrol the sunken crypts, and leeches lurk in the murky waters. Poison resistance mead is absolutely mandatory before stepping foot in this biome.
